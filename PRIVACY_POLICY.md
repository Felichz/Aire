# Privacy Policy for Aire

**Last Updated: February 17, 2026**

Aire (the "App") is a simple breathing exercise application designed to prioritize user privacy. This Privacy Policy explains our practices regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of information.

## 1. Information Collection and Use
**Aire does NOT collect, store, or share any personal information.** 
We do not track your identity, your location, or your usage habits. All data related to your breathing exercises (durations, repetitions) is stored exclusively on your own device and is never transmitted to us or any third party.

## 2. Local Storage
The App uses local storage (AsyncStorage) on your device to remember your preferred breathing durations and cycle settings. This data stays on your phone and is deleted if you uninstall the App.

## 3. Third-Party Services
The App does not use third-party analytics, advertising networks, or tracking tools.

## 4. Children's Privacy
Since Aire does not collect any personal information, it is safe for users of all ages, including children under 13.
